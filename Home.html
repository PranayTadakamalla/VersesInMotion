Featured Poems

The Way I Miss You

I miss you when the sun shines bright,
And even more in silent night.
I miss the thought of seeing you the next day,
The little things I used to do to make you stay.

Whispers of Longing

In quiet moments, echoes stay,
Of words we spoke that slipped away.
I close my eyes and hear your tone,
A whisper soft, yet all alone.

Fading Footsteps

The paths we walked now stand so still,
The echoes lost against my will.
Each step we took, so full of light,
Now fades into the endless night.

A Love Unsaid

The words I never dared to tell,
Still linger where my heart once fell.
If time could give a second chance,
I'd weave them into one last dance.

Echoes of You

The stars above still spell your name,
Though time has blurred the burning flame.
I trace the sky and wish you knew,
That every echo leads to you.

Unfinished Symphony

A melody left incomplete,
A song that plays but never meets.
Your voice remains, a haunting tune,
Beneath the silver light of moon.
